In order to create my workwear collection I went through the process of creating a trend board, mood board, concept board, consumer profile as well as a color & fabric story. These boards were critical in forming the framework of my collection and set me up to transition right into the garment development phase. I utilized Adobe Illustrator and upon the completion of this collection gave a full presentation about my design process.
